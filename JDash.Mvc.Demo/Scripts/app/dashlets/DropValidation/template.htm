Disable drop

Check to disable dropping of all dashlets in this dashboard.

Make sticky

When a dashlet is marked as Sticky, users cannot drag dashlet and dropping of other dashlets in front of stricky dashlet is disabled.

This can be very handy for your dashlets you want to mention to the user.

Track events

To have a try, drag a dashlet. Events will be logged below automatically.

Events also include current and target position of dragged dashlet, which lets you to have full control over the operation. You can cancel dragging by setting Cancel property of event.
